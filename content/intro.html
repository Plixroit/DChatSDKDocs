DChat SDK

A self-sovereign, end-to-end encrypted messaging SDK built on a Cosmos-based blockchain. No phone numbers, no central servers - just cryptographic identities and decentralised delivery.

E2EE by Default
Every message is encrypted with the recipient's Secp256k1 key before leaving the device.
Blockchain Identity
Identities are BIP39/BIP44 key pairs registered on-chain - no email, no phone required.
gRPC Streaming
Bidirectional gRPC stream with automatic reconnect, ACK queue, and offline message retry.

What is the DChat SDK?

DChatSDK is a .NET class library that gives your application a single entry point - IDChatSDKClient - to manage the full lifecycle of decentralised, encrypted messaging:

  • Generate and restore blockchain identities from a BIP39 seed phrase.
  • Encrypt and decrypt messages with rotating E2EE key pairs (forward secrecy).
  • Send typed messages: text, images, video, audio, files, albums, location, and more.
  • Manage group chats with symmetric sender keys and automatic key rotation.
  • WebRTC peer-to-peer calls without a signalling server.
  • Stream messages in real time over a persistent gRPC connection.
